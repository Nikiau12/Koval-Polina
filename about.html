About me

Graduated from the Higher School of Economics, Faculty of Animation. She has been making handmade clothes for about three years. Polina also paints on fabrics and canvases, works with wood and ceramics.

Polina's works are inspired by women's bodies, nature and have religious overtones. The paintings depict naked women in the garden, angels, magical animals and imaginary, mystical flowers. The artist often paints on plywood or pieces of wood found on the streets. Now living in Tbilisi, Polina creates street art objects. She likes to revitalize and ‘liberate’ the works so that they continue to exist autonomously and change without further influence from the author.
